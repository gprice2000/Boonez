Features

Boonez is a platform for Appalachian State students to connect academically and socially and for local businesses to advertise. Students can sign up with a personal account and gain access to a personalized dashboard, a public and private calendar to keep your life organized, the ability to find friends through your course numbers, a messaging system to talk with your friends, and the ability to view local advertisements.

Business accounts also have a personalized dashboard along with the ability to create and manage advertisements. Want to tell the community you have a big sale? You can!
